Energy Consumption Analysis

Website and Analysis completed by Megan Gray for UNCC Bootcamp Data Analytics Bootcamp, Unit 11.

Energy Consumption Analysis between 4 Power Plants in the Ohio River Valley, and Northeastern Illinois.

The analysis reflected in this website was completed on a Kaggle.com dataset, which was compiled by PJM Interconnection LLC (PJM), and submitted to Kaggle.com by Rob Mulla. The dataset itself has hourly consumption rates of power recorded in MegaWatts for 12 power plants that are located throughout the Ohio River Valley, Pennsylvania, and Northeastern Illinois. For this analysis, the first 4 power plants were chosen and they are American Electric Power (AEP), Commonwealth Edison (COMED), Dayton Power and Light Company (DAYTON), Duke Energy of Ohio and Kentucky (DEOK).
